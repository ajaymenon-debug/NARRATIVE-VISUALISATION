Oil Pipeline Accidents

An interactive dashboard for the analysis of Oil Pipeline Accidents in different parts of the world

The number of Oil Pipeline Accidents around the world are too high and we hear about so much losses due to these accidents. Huge amount of money is lost in each of these accidents and there are even fatalities that occur during these accidents.

 Things which can be derived from this dashboard

  • Find the locations where maximum accidents have occured
  • Find the number of accidents in each year from 2010 - 2017 
  • Understand what is main cause for Oil Pipeline Accidents 
  • Understand when and where the maximum cost is spend during the accidents 
  • Understand what cause of the accident gives maximum cost spend
